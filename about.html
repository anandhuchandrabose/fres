Our Story

It all began with a simple truth—nothing beats home-cooked food. We all leave home at some point chasing dreams, careers, or life.

On the other end, home chefs cook with soul, while self-help groups like Kudumbashree nurture fresh, chemical-free ingredients from every corner of the country.

Connecting the dots, together we are building a value chain: they bring the soul and expertise, we bring the technology & reach.

Our Promise

We deliver what your kitchen would—if it could.

Mission

To build an ecosystem that connects small local entrepreneurs with consumers, promoting transparency, authenticity, and empowering economic enfranchisement.

Vision

To reimagine food delivery by building a people-powered platform that connects hearts, through homemade delights at scale, with soul.

Values

  • Community over corporations
  • Zero-waste, zero-plastic, zero-guilt
  • Simple, honest, soulful
  • Transparent always

What are we doing?

At FresMo, we connect passionate home chefs with self-help groups like Kudumbashree to source fresh, high-quality ingredients, so every meal we serve feels truly homemade.

Because food should nourish your soul and empower those who make it, we’re serving the experience of control over what’s cooked, how it’s made, and who’s making it.

You must be 18 years or older to deliver with FresMo in Kerala.

Ride a reliable bike, scooter, or bicycle—whatever keeps you quick in Trivandrum traffic.

Documentation

Keep a valid driver's license or government-issued ID handy, and share your Social Security number for background verification.

▶ FAQs

Answers before you hit the road

How do I get started with FresMo in Trivandrum?

Sign up through our website, complete the background check, and once you are approved, you will receive your FresMo delivery kit to begin.

What areas does FresMo serve in Trivandrum?

FresMo operates across key neighbourhoods in and around Technopark, Kazhakoottam, and city-centre routes. Check the service map in the app for live coverage.

How are deliveries assigned?

Deliveries match live demand with your zone preferences. High-performing partners get first access to priority batches and pre-booked orders.

Is there a dress code?

No strict uniforms—just stay neat, professional, and use the FresMo delivery kit for a consistent customer experience.

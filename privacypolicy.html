Privacy Policy

 

FresMo collects and processes personal information to provide and enhance its services. This includes data such as your name, contact details, location, and payment information. Terms and Conditions

 

Upon placing an order, FresMo confirms its acceptance via email or SMS. In certain situations, such as technical issues or typographical errors leading to incorrect pricing, FresMo reserves the right to cancel orders. Additionally, if an order is canceled after confirmation, FresMo may charge a cancellation fee ranging from INR 50 up to the full order value. This fee compensates restaurant and delivery partners for their efforts.

 

Cancellation and Refund Policy

 

As a general rule, once an order is confirmed, it cannot be canceled by the user. If you attempt to cancel after confirmation, FresMo may charge a cancellation fee as mentioned above. However, if an item in your order is unavailable, FresMo will inform you, allowing you to cancel the entire order without incurring a fee. Refunds are processed in specific situations, such as:

 

•               If the order packaging is tampered with or damaged at the time of delivery.

•               If FresMo cancels your order due to delivery location constraints.

•               If you cancel the order at the time of confirmation due to unavailability of the ordered items.

 

Refunds are credited to your account within 7 business days, depending on your payment method.

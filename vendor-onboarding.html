What are the requirements to join FresMo as a home chef?

Bring your passion for authentic Kerala dishes, comply with local food-safety norms, and keep documents such as your FSSAI registration and recent water test results ready.

How does FresMo assist in promoting my dishes?

We champion your menu through targeted social promotions, featured listings, and community storytelling to spotlight your craft.

We maintain a standard price chart to ensure consistent value for customers while celebrating each chef’s uniqueness, keeping pricing transparent and fair.
